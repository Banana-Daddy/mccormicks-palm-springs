Our Story

Keith Crossed
An Ocean.

Back in 1981, Keith McCormick was a successful entrepreneur in the United Kingdom. By 1985, he'd relocated to the California desert and hosted his first exotic car auction in Palm Springs.

Forty-one years later, the family still runs the floor. Two auctions a year. A year-round showroom. The same well-documented cars that built the reputation.
