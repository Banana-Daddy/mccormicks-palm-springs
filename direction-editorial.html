Our Story

Keith McCormick crossed an ocean and never stopped collecting.

Back in 1981, Keith McCormick was a successful entrepreneur in the United Kingdom. By 1985, he had relocated to the California desert and hosted his first exotic car auction in Palm Springs.

Forty-one years later, the family still runs the floor. Two auctions a year. A year-round showroom. The same well-documented cars that built the reputation.
